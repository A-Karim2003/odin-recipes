Spaghetti Bolognese

What is spaghetti bolognese

Spaghetti bolognese consists of spaghetti (long strings of pasta) with an Italian ragù
(meat sauce) made with minced beef and tomatoes, served with Parmesan cheese.
Spaghetti bolognese is one of the most popular pasta dishes eaten outside of Italy.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 rashers smoked streaky beef, finely chopped
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, trimmed and finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 2-3 sprigs rosemary leaves picked and finely chopped
  • 500g beef mince

For the bolognese sauce

  • 2 x 400g tins plum tomatoes
  • small pack basil leaves picked, ¾ finely chopped
    and the rest left whole for garnish
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 fresh bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 1 red chilli deseeded and finely chopped (optional)
  • 6 cherry tomatoes sliced in half

Steps for making spaghetti-bolognese

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Fry for 10 minutes, or until golden and crisp, with 4 finely chopped beef rashers.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and add the 2 onions, 2 carrots, 2 celery sticks, 2 garlic cloves,
    and the leaves from 2-3 rosemary sprigs, all finely chopped, and cook for 10 minutes. Stir
    often until the vegetables soften.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high, add 500g beef mince, and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring
    occasionally, until the meat has browned all over.
  5. Add 2 tins plum tomatoes, the finely chopped leaves from ¾ small pack basil, 1 tsp dried oregano,
    2 bay leaves, 2 tbsp tomato purée, 1 beef stock cube, 1 deseeded and finely chopped red chilli
    (if using)and 6 halved cherry tomatoes. With a wooden spoon, break up the plum tomatoes.
  6. Bring to a boil, then lower to a slow simmer and cover. Cook for 1 hour 15 minutes, stirring regularly,
    until the sauce is rich and thick.
  7. Check the seasoning and mix in the 75g shredded parmesan.
  8. Cook 400g spaghetti when the bolognese is almost done.
  9. Drain the pasta and either mix it with the bolognese sauce or serve it on top.
